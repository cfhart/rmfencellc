About Us

Our Story

Founded in 2022, we are a proud family-owned business based in Belmont, NC. With deep roots in our community, we are dedicated to providing top-notch fencing solutions to homeowners and businesses within a 200-mile radius.

Our Mission

Here at RM FENCE LLC we are dedicated to providing quality fencing solutions which incorporate both exceptional craftsmanship and amazing customer service. Our mission is to help enhance your property's security, privacy, and overall visual appeal while ensuring sure that every job is completed to the highest standards of excellence and reliability.

Please contact us for any inquiries, questions, or to schedule your free consultation. We look forward to helping you with all your fencing needs.
